Case 03

Funding Access

Norsken — Funding Access for Founders

The Problem

There's money everywhere for early-stage entrepreneurs — grants, subsidies, EU programs, innovation vouchers. Billions sitting in pots. Almost nobody gets it.

Not because they don't qualify. Because finding it is a full-time job. The EU alone has hundreds of funding programs, each with different eligibility criteria, application windows, and reporting requirements.

For entrepreneurs: you're entitled to support that could change your trajectory, but discovering it requires expertise you don't have. For funding bodies: you're buried in administration while founders who need help never apply. For the economy: capital sits idle, talent goes unfunded. Pure waste.

The Challenge

Build a solution that gets funding to the founders who need it — faster, with less friction, for everyone involved.

01 The founder side — help entrepreneurs discover what they're eligible for, match them to programs, reduce application pain
02 The agency side — help funding bodies process and distribute more efficiently
03 The system side — reimagine how eligibility, matching, or distribution could work if designed today

Constraints

Real-world messiness. Funding programs are fragmented, poorly documented, constantly changing. Work with imperfect information.
Trust matters. Founders have been burned by consultants who overpromise. Agencies are cautious about automation.
Scope to one slice. Pick one user, one problem, one interaction — and make it work.

Potential Use Cases

Grant Matcher
Answer 10 questions about your startup, get a ranked list of every grant and subsidy you qualify for
Application Generator
AI drafts your grant application based on your pitch deck and the program's requirements
Deadline Radar
Track application windows across programs and get alerts before they close
Agency Inbox Cleaner
Help funding bodies triage and pre-qualify applications automatically
