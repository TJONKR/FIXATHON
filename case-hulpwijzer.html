Case 01

Hulpwijzer

in collaboration with Single Super Mom

The Problem

Thousands of single mothers are missing out on financial support they're entitled to — not because it doesn't exist, but because finding it is nearly impossible.

Financial aid is scattered across hundreds of municipalities, funds, and schemes. Each has its own rules, forms, and eligibility criteria. For someone already stretched thin — working, raising kids, surviving — navigating this maze isn't just hard. It's designed to fail them.

The Toeslagenaffaire made it worse. Trust in government systems is broken. Many who need help the most now avoid the system entirely.

The Challenge

Build an AI-powered solution that helps single mothers discover and access the financial support they're entitled to.

01 Aggregation — bring fragmented information from multiple sources into one place
02 Accessibility — make the interface human and approachable for people who distrust bureaucratic systems
03 Automation with oversight — relieve municipalities from paper-heavy workflows while keeping humans in the loop

Constraints

Human-in-the-loop is mandatory. Full automation without oversight is not acceptable. Design for verification, not replacement.
Trust is fragile. Your UX choices matter. Assume your user has been burned by "the system" before.
Scope realistically. A working prototype that solves one piece well beats a grand vision that doesn't run.

Potential Use Cases

Eligibility Scanner
Upload your situation once, get matched to all schemes you qualify for across municipalities
Application Assistant
AI-guided form filling that pre-populates fields and explains requirements in plain language
Deadline Tracker
Never miss a renewal or application window — proactive alerts for all your active benefits
Municipality Dashboard
Help case workers see the full picture of a family's situation across fragmented systems
